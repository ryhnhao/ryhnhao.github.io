About Me

I am a Research Assistant at the Big Data Intelligence Lab, Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Zaiqing Nie. Prior to this, I obtained the MPhil degree from Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Biqing Huang, and obtained the BEng degree (with Hons.) from Shenyuan Honors College, Beihang University, working closely with Prof. Ying Cheng and Prof. Fei Tao. Additionally, I once interned at SenseTime Research, working closely with Dr. Jiang Wu.

Research Interests: The automatic system is an exciting and popular topic. My research interests involve the vision perception algorithms and decision-making in autonomous systems. It is exhilarating to be able to contribute to this field.
